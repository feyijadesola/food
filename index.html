About Us

Betty foods is one of the fastest growing Quick Service Restaurant in Nigeria. It was founded in July 1993 and since then has played pace setting role in Restaurant Services. Betty foods has indeed grown across lagos state and the country, Nigeria as a whole. Betty foods is an indigenous company, our meals are prepared using authentic Afica and international spices flavoured with love. Our most popular meal is our fresh Rostisserie Chicken with spicy Rice, the second most popular meal is our chickwhizz; freshly roasted sandwich with chunky pieces of spicy fried chicken and crisp vegetables coupled with africa dishes such as amala, efo riro, semovita, afang soup, ofada rice, white soup, pounded yam, etc we serve over two hundred customers monthly. we treat our customers with care and love
